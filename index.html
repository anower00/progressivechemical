About

PROGRESSIVE CHEMICAL INDUSTRY LIMITED is located in Tianjin, the largest chemical export harbor and biggest and industrial city in north China. We also have sales and distribution offices in Bangladesh, Qatar & KSA. We have been in exportation field with over 12 years. We offer a wide range of Inudustrial Chemicals, Raw materials and Auxiliaries as well as Petrochemicals including White Mineral Oil (Liquid Paraffin). Now we are a leading professional Chemical exporter as well as other products like White Mineral Oil, Petrochemicals, Dyestuff, Textile and Detergent Chemical including food additives.
